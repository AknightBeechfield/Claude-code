Beechfield Collection

Vintage

Built new. Worn-in from day one.

Character you can't fake — except we did.

The Vintage collection takes classic silhouettes and adds a patina of character — washed fabrics, distressed finishes, faded tones. The kind of look that takes years to achieve with a regular cap, achieved from first wear.

It's not about looking old. It's about looking lived-in — effortless, individual, and ready for decoration that sits naturally into the fabric rather than sitting on top of it.

Washed. Distressed. Decoration-ready.

Vintage fabrics present a unique opportunity for decorators — embroidery takes on a softer, more integrated look when applied to washed cotton. Screen prints sit with a faded, authentic finish that customers love.

  • Washed and enzyme-treated cotton
  • Low-profile and unstructured fits
  • Distressed peak and panel finishing
  • Faded, tonal colourways
  • Compatible with all major decoration methods

Who it's for

Streetwear brands

Vintage styles sit naturally alongside contemporary streetwear aesthetics — the worn-in look adds authenticity to brand-led decoration without extra effort.

Lifestyle retailers

Customers drawn to a relaxed, effortless aesthetic find Vintage styles immediately appealing. The organic, faded tones cross seasonal colour trends easily.

Fashion-forward decorators

Decorators who want to offer something beyond the standard structured cap — Vintage gives their customers a point of difference that's immediately visible.
