Why Beechfield

The original authority in rebrandable headwear. Here's why decorators and promotional buyers trust us.

30 Years of Expertise

Leading the rebrandable headwear market since 1994. Three decades of cap knowledge, trusted by decorators worldwide.

Designed for Decoration

Every product engineered for embroidery, patches, transfer, screen print and DTG. Clean panels, stable substrates, consistent results.

Always in Stock

Make-to-stock model with daily inventory updates. The styles you rely on, ready when you need them.

60+ Colours

From classic black and navy to seasonal pastels and neons. The broadest rebrandable colour palette in the industry.

Responsible by Design

Decorator Academy

Designed for Decoration

Every Beechfield product is engineered with decoration in mind. Explore techniques, placement guides, and expert tutorials.

Embroidery

The most popular decoration method for headwear. Clean surfaces and stable panels for precise stitching.

Patches

Woven, PVC, and leather patches. Versatile placement options across front, side, and back panels.

Transfer

Heat transfer for full-colour designs. Compatible with our flat-panel and structured cap styles.

Screen Print

Bold, durable prints on flat surfaces. Ideal for promotional runs and event headwear.
