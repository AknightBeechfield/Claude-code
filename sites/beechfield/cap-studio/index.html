Cap Studio

Find the perfect cap for your brief. Every time.

Choose with confidence

Three variables. Thousands of combinations. Cap Studio narrows the field to the styles that matter for your brief.

Decoration technique

Whether you embroider, screen print, transfer or all-over print, Cap Studio surfaces the styles built for your method. Crown construction, panel stability and fabric weight all affect the outcome.

Silhouette & structure

5-panel, 6-panel, structured, unstructured, snapback or trucker — the silhouette determines decoration placement, fit and the price range your customer expects.

End use & customer

Sports, corporate, fashion or workwear — the intended use drives fabric weight, certification needs and colour range expectations. Get to the right cap faster.
