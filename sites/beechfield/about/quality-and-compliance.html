Quality & Compliance

Every product tested, every standard met. How Beechfield ensures consistent quality across the range.

Quality You Can Rely On

For decorators, consistency is everything. A front panel that varies in stiffness from batch to batch means embroidery quality varies too. Fabric weight that shifts between seasons means print parameters have to be re-tested every time.

Beechfield invests in raw material testing, construction audits and finished product inspection to ensure that when you set up a cap on your embroidery machine or heat press, the result is the same every single time.

Our Quality Pillars

Three disciplines that underpin every Beechfield product

Material Testing

Every fabric is tested for weight, shrinkage, colour fastness and pilling resistance before it enters production. We test to BS EN ISO standards — the same benchmarks used across the European textile industry.

Pre-Production

Production Audits

In-line inspections during manufacturing check seam integrity, panel alignment, peak curvature and hardware durability. Every batch is audited against Beechfield's master specifications before it ships.

In-Production

AQL Inspection

Finished goods are inspected using Acceptable Quality Level (AQL) sampling. We apply AQL 2.5 for critical defects and AQL 4.0 for minor defects — tighter than most promotional product standards.

Post-Production

Certifications

Independent, third-party verified standards

OCS

Organic Content Standard

Verifies the organic cotton content in our EarthAware range. Chain of custody tracked from raw fibre to finished product.

GRS

Global Recycled Standard

Certifies the recycled polyester content in products like the Recycled Original Cuffed Beanie (B45R) and Recycled Pro-Style Cap (B70R).

100

OEKO-TEX Standard 100

Tested for harmful substances. Confirms that textiles are safe for human use — important for products worn directly against skin.

CE

Regulatory Compliance

All products meet EU and UK regulatory requirements including REACH, product safety directives and appropriate labelling standards.

Decoration-Ready Quality Control

Standard textile quality is necessary but not sufficient. Because Beechfield products are designed for decoration, we test additional parameters that matter specifically to decorators:

Panel tension — front panels are tested for consistent stiffness so embroidery machines produce uniform results across a batch.

Colour consistency — dye lots are checked against Pantone references to ensure colour accuracy, critical for corporate branding programmes.

Tear-away labels — most Beechfield products feature tear-away care labels, allowing clean re-labelling for private-label programmes.
