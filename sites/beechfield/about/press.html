Brands in the Beechfield Brands group

Beechfield is part of the Beechfield Brands group, alongside BagBase, Quadra and Westford Mill. Together, the group offers one of the widest ranges of decoration-ready headwear, bags and textile accessories in the European promotional products market. All products are sold exclusively through authorised distributors.
