EarthAware® Organic Cotton Bucket Hat

OCS-certified organic cotton. The same decoration performance. A lower impact.

Find a Distributor

The Product

Fashion-led silhouette. Organic backbone.

The B90N takes the bucket hat silhouette — one of the most in-demand shapes across fashion, festival and outdoor markets — and builds it in 100% organic cotton certified to the Organic Content Standard. The wide, structured brim provides generous decoration real estate on four separate panels, and the unstructured crown accepts embroidery, patches and screen print with equal confidence.

Available in a seasonal palette anchored by natural, earthy tones, the B90N is built for programmes where sustainability is a brief requirement, not an afterthought.

Built for Decoration

Four panels. Versatile placement. One organic blank.

Embroidery

The six-panel crown construction provides a stable, flat surface on each panel. The organic cotton weave holds embroidery thread cleanly — machine backing works without distortion across all standard stitch counts.

Patches & Labels

Woven, rubber and PVC patches bond cleanly to the unstructured panels. The four discrete crown sections give flexible placement options — front, side or back panel without crowding the brim decoration area.

Screen Print & Transfer

The flat panel construction and consistent cotton weave deliver predictable ink and heat transfer adhesion. The brim offers an additional print zone for smaller brand hits or repeat patterns.
