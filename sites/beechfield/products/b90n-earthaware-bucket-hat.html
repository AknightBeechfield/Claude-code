The Story

Same shapes. Lower footprint.

The EarthAware® collection reworks the classic Beechfield shapes you know in 100% organic cotton, certified to OCS standards. Same decoration-ready construction. Same reliability. Lower environmental impact. The B90N Bucket Hat is the collection’s standout silhouette — a fashion-led shape that’s moved from trend to wardrobe staple.

OCS certification gives decorators and their customers a credible, third-party-verified sustainability claim — without compromising on product quality or decoration performance. Every EarthAware® product is independently verified from raw fibre through to finished headwear.

Natural tones and earthy colourways make the B90N a natural fit for lifestyle, outdoor and wellness brands. The wide brim delivers UPF50+ sun protection, while the unstructured crown and ventilation eyelets keep the wearer comfortable in warmer conditions. TearAway labelling means your client’s brand is the only one the wearer sees.
