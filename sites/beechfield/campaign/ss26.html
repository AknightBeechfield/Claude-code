Key Trends for SS26

Three defining trends shaping the season — each one reflected across our product range and ready for your branding.

Organic Cotton

Sustainability is no longer a niche — it is the expectation. Our EarthAware collection leads with GOTS-certified organic cotton, offering decorators a responsibly sourced blank that does not compromise on decoration performance.

New additions for SS26 include the B26N Studio Cap and B90N Bucket Hat, both available in soft, wearable colourways designed to sell through summer and beyond. Every EarthAware product carries certified organic traceability from field to finished garment.

Explore EarthAware

Cord Textures

Corduroy is back in a big way. Textured fabrics add a premium, tactile feel to headwear that stands out on the shelf and in the hand. Cord caps and bucket hats bring a vintage-meets-modern aesthetic that resonates across demographics.

Available in classic earthy tones and updated seasonal shades, cord textures pair beautifully with embroidery and tonal branding for an elevated, understated look. The tactile quality gives decorated products a premium perceived value that flat-panel cotton simply cannot match.

Shop Cord Styles
Cord Textures

Vintage Wash

The worn-in, lived-in look continues to dominate streetwear and lifestyle branding. Vintage wash caps deliver that pre-loved character straight out of the box — ideal for brands that want an authentic, relaxed identity.

The B655 Low Profile Vintage Cap is a standout, with a soft hand-feel and low-profile silhouette that flatters every wearer. Perfect for embroidery, patch and direct-to-garment print. Pair it with a vintage wash bucket hat for a coordinated summer collection.
