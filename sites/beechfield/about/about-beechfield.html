About / Our Story

Original Headwear Since 1994

Over thirty years of craft, credibility and headwear built for the decoration trade. From a single idea in Wigan to pan-European distribution, Beechfield remains the go-to name in promotional and retail-ready headwear.

The Beechfield Story

Born in Wigan. Built for decorators.

Beechfield was founded in 1994 in Wigan, England, with a straightforward ambition: to create headwear purpose-built for the promotional products industry. At a time when caps and beanies were often an afterthought in distributor catalogues, Beechfield saw an opportunity to bring real quality, consistency and decoration-readiness to the category.

From day one, every product has been designed with the end decorator in mind. Reinforced front panels for clean embroidery. Tear-away labels for seamless rebranding. Consistent sizing and construction, batch after batch. These are not afterthoughts; they are the foundation of everything we do.

Today, Beechfield is part of Beechfield Brands Ltd, a group that also includes BagBase, Quadra and Westford Mill. Together, these four brands supply headwear, bags and accessories to distributors, decorators and promotional merchandise companies across more than 40 countries.

Three Decades of Headwear Heritage

1994

Founded in Wigan

Beechfield is established in Wigan, England, with a clear mission: create headwear that the promotional products trade can truly rely on for quality and decorability.

Early 2000s

Range Expansion

The range grows beyond caps into beanies, bucket hats and winter accessories. Key product families emerge: the Originals collection and the Snowstar beanie range become staples of the distributor catalogue.

Mid 2000s

Pan-European Growth

Beechfield expands from UK-focused distribution to a pan-European network, partnering with leading promotional product distributors across the continent.

2008

Decoration-Ready Design

The introduction of tear-away labels, reinforced front panels and flat embroidery areas marks a turning point. Every product is now explicitly engineered for the decorator.

2012

Vintage & Urbanwear

The Vintage and Urbanwear collections launch, bringing fashion-forward silhouettes to the promotional channel. Dad caps, flat peaks and washed finishes find a new audience.

2015

Morf Multi-Use

The Morf neckwear family debuts, offering multi-functional tubular accessories that can be worn as a snood, headband, face covering or beanie. An instant best-seller.

2018

EarthAware Eco Collection

The EarthAware range launches with OCS-certified organic cotton and recycled polyester products. Beechfield becomes one of the first headwear brands in the industry to offer a dedicated sustainable collection.

2020s

Digital Transformation

New digital tools, the MediaHub asset platform, online lookbooks and the Decorator Academy make it easier than ever for trade partners to work with the brand.

2024

30 Years Strong

Beechfield celebrates three decades in the headwear trade. With over 500 styles and colours, distribution in 40+ countries and a reputation built on reliability, the brand enters its next chapter.

What Original Headwear Means

Built to carry someone else's story.

Original does not mean first. It means authentic. Every product we make is designed to be decorated — to carry a story that is not ours. We build headwear that becomes someone else's brand, someone else's identity, someone else's promotional campaign.

That has been the principle since day one. Whether it is a five-panel cap for a corporate event, a cuffed beanie for a streetwear label, or a bucket hat for a festival, Beechfield products are the blank canvas the industry depends on. Consistent quality. True colour matching. Decoration-ready construction. Every time.

Our Design Process

With over 30 years of headwear expertise, every Beechfield product follows a rigorous development journey from concept to delivery.

1

Design

Our in-house team identifies trends, analyses market needs and sketches new styles — always with decoration in mind from the first pencil stroke.

2

Prototype

Initial samples are created and refined. Fabrics, trims, construction methods and colourways are evaluated against our standards for the promotional trade.

3

Test

Prototypes undergo embroidery, print and transfer trials. We test tear-away label removal, wash durability and colour consistency across production runs.

4

Produce

Approved styles enter full production with our manufacturing partners, who follow detailed specifications to ensure batch-to-batch consistency.

5

Quality Check

Every shipment is inspected for construction quality, colour accuracy, sizing and compliance. Products that do not meet our standards do not reach the warehouse.

6

Ship

Approved stock is delivered to our European distribution centres, ready for rapid despatch to our network of distributors and decorators.

Designed for Decoration

Every detail serves the decorator.

Beechfield products are not simply blank garments. They are engineered for the decoration process. Reinforced front panels provide a stable, smooth surface for embroidery. Tear-away labels allow for complete rebranding. Flat seam construction avoids hoop marks. Consistent dye lots mean the colour your client approves is the colour they receive.

Whether you specialise in embroidery, heat transfer, screen print, sublimation or woven patches, Beechfield headwear is built to deliver clean, professional results every time.

Our Product Families

From core classics to fashion-forward seasonal pieces, each Beechfield collection is designed to meet a specific need in the promotional and retail markets.

Originals

The foundation of the range. Classic silhouettes in the widest colour palette, built for high-volume decoration programmes. Includes the iconic B15, B45 and B65 families.

EarthAware

Our certified sustainable collection. OCS-certified organic cotton and recycled polyester styles for brands that need to demonstrate environmental responsibility without compromising on quality.

Vintage

Washed finishes, low-profile shapes and heritage-inspired details. The Vintage collection brings a lived-in, authentic aesthetic that resonates with fashion-conscious end users.

Urbanwear

Street-influenced silhouettes for the brand-conscious market. Flat peaks, snapbacks and structured shapes that cater to music, sport and lifestyle brands.

Our Sustainability Commitment

Responsible choices are woven into every stage of our supply chain. We are committed to reducing our environmental impact while maintaining the quality and performance that decorators depend on.

OCS

OCS Certified Organic Cotton

Our EarthAware collection features products made from organic cotton certified to the Organic Content Standard (OCS). This third-party verification ensures the organic fibre content is tracked from source to finished product, giving our distributors and their clients full confidence in sustainability claims.

♻

Recycled Polyester

An increasing number of Beechfield styles incorporate recycled polyester made from post-consumer plastic bottles. This diverts waste from landfill, reduces reliance on virgin petroleum-based fibres and lowers the carbon footprint of each product — all without sacrificing durability or decoration performance.

☘

Organic Cotton

Organic cotton is grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilisers, using methods that support soil health and biodiversity. Across our range, we continue to expand the proportion of styles available in organic cotton to meet growing demand from environmentally conscious brand owners.

✉

Responsible Packaging

We are actively working to reduce unnecessary packaging across our supply chain. This includes transitioning to recycled and recyclable polybags, reducing plastic content per unit and eliminating single-use packaging elements wherever possible.

Our Partner Network

Beechfield products are available exclusively through the promotional products trade. We work with a carefully selected network of distributors who share our commitment to quality and service.

We do not sell direct to consumers. Beechfield products reach the market through a pan-European network of specialist promotional merchandise distributors and wholesale partners. This trade-only model ensures that decorators and resellers always have access to competitive pricing, dedicated support and expert product knowledge.

Our distribution partners carry Beechfield stock in their own warehouses across Europe, meaning short lead times and local availability for end customers. Whether a decorator needs 50 beanies for a boutique brand or 10,000 caps for a national campaign, the Beechfield supply chain is built to deliver.
