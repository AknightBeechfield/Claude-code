About / Responsible Choices

Responsible Choices

Not perfect. But honest, and getting better.

01

Responsible Sourcing

Suppliers chosen on more than price.

We choose suppliers based on quality, compliance and conditions — not just price. That means factory audits, ethical trading standards, and long-term relationships with manufacturers who share our values.

02

Responsible Materials

Better fibres where we can. Working on the rest.

Where we can, we use organic, recycled or lower-impact materials. Where we can't yet, we're working on it. Our EarthAware® range covers OCS-certified organic cotton and GRS-certified recycled polyester — and it keeps growing.

03

Responsible Packaging

Less plastic. More sense.

Polybag-free where possible. Recycled content where not. We've been systematically reviewing our packaging across the range — reducing single-use plastic and switching to recycled or recyclable alternatives.
