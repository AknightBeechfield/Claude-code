Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Beechfield products, ordering and decoration.

30 Years of Expertise Designed for Decoration Always in Stock 60+ Colours Responsible by Design

Products & Range

We offer caps, beanies, bucket hats, visors, performance headwear and accessories. Our range includes over 100 styles in 60+ colours, designed specifically for decoration.

Yes — every Beechfield product is engineered with decoration in mind. Clean front panels, stable substrates and consistent fabric weights ensure your branding looks great, whether you embroider, print, apply patches or use heat transfer.

EarthAware is our sustainable range featuring organic cotton and recycled polyester products. All EarthAware styles are OEKO-TEX certified and designed to meet the growing demand for responsible headwear.

Yes, we have a dedicated kids range available in many of our popular styles. Check individual product pages for size availability.

Ordering & Distribution

Beechfield products are available exclusively through our network of authorised distributors. Visit our Where to Buy page to find your nearest distributor or use our Inventory Finder to check stock availability.

Minimum order quantities vary by distributor. Contact your local distributor directly for their specific terms and pricing.

We operate a make-to-stock model, which means our core range is always available. We update inventory daily across our distributor network. Use our Inventory Finder for real-time availability.

We distribute through authorised partners across Europe. For international enquiries outside our current network, please contact us directly.

Decoration & Techniques

Our products support embroidery, patches (woven, PVC, leather), heat transfer, screen print, DTG (direct-to-garment), sublimation, deboss, foil and laser techniques. Visit our Decorator Academy for technique-specific guidance.

Decoration placement guides and technical specifications are available on each product page and through our Decorator Academy resources section.

We don't decorate products ourselves — that's what our amazing decorator community does! We design the blanks to ensure your decoration looks its best.

Quality & Sustainability

Yes. All products undergo rigorous quality testing including colourfastness, wash durability, fabric weight consistency and construction standards. We're committed to delivering the same quality, batch after batch.

Our products are OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified. The EarthAware range uses certified organic cotton and GRS-certified recycled polyester.
