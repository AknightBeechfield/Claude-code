Better Materials. Honest Claims.

Organic cotton, recycled polyester, certified supply chains. No greenwash. Just the facts.

EarthAware and Beyond

Where we can, we use organic, recycled or lower-impact materials. Where we cannot yet, we are working on it. The EarthAware collection brings together our certified sustainable products — OCS organic cotton, GRS recycled polyester, OEKO-TEX tested fabrics.

We do not over-celebrate the basics. Polybag-free where possible. Recycled content where not. We choose suppliers based on quality, compliance and conditions — not just price. That is the honest position.

Why Beechfield for Sustainable Headwear?

Certified, Not Just Claimed

Every sustainability claim in the EarthAware range is backed by third-party certification. OCS verifies our organic cotton content. GRS certifies our recycled polyester. OEKO-TEX confirms product safety. These are not marketing terms — they are independently audited standards with chain-of-custody tracking from raw material to finished product.

No Compromise on Quality

Sustainable materials should not mean inferior products. EarthAware headwear delivers the same construction quality, colour consistency and decoration performance as the conventional range. Organic cotton caps embroider just as cleanly. Recycled polyester beanies hold colour just as well. Sustainability is an upgrade to the material, not a downgrade to the product.

Growing Range, Honest Roadmap

We cannot make every product sustainable overnight. The EarthAware collection currently covers core styles — 5-panel caps, beanies, bucket hats and studio caps — in organic cotton and recycled polyester. The range grows each season. We are transparent about what is certified today and what is still in development.

Certifications

OCS — Organic Content Standard

Verifies the presence and amount of organic material in a product. Tracked from source to final product.

GRS — Global Recycled Standard

Certifies recycled content and responsible production. Verified recycled polyester in our performance fabrics.

OEKO-TEX Standard 100

Tests for harmful substances. Confirms products are safe for human use. Applied across key product lines.

What to Look For in Sustainable Headwear

Navigating sustainability claims in the promotional products industry requires understanding what the certifications actually mean.

Organic Cotton vs Conventional

Organic cotton is grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilisers, using less water through rain-fed farming practices. OCS certification verifies the organic content from farm to factory. For clients with sustainability policies, OCS-certified organic cotton headwear provides a verifiable claim they can report on — not just a material swap with no proof.

OCS certified Rain-fed farming Traceable

Recycled Polyester

GRS-certified recycled polyester diverts plastic waste from landfill and reduces dependence on virgin petroleum. In headwear, recycled polyester performs identically to conventional polyester — same durability, same wash performance, same decoration compatibility. The difference is in the supply chain, not the finished product. Ideal for performance caps where cotton is not suitable.

GRS certified Diverted waste Performance equal

Packaging and Polybags

Sustainability is not just about the product — it extends to how it reaches the customer. EarthAware products ship polybag-free where possible, and where packaging is required, recycled or recyclable materials are used. For decorators, this reduces the waste generated in the branding process and supports closed-loop reporting for end-clients with ESG commitments.

Decoration Guide for Sustainable Headwear

Organic cotton caps and beanies decorate identically to their conventional equivalents — embroidery tension, print adhesion and transfer performance are unchanged. The key difference is in the story you can tell alongside the decoration. Clients increasingly want their branded headwear to carry a sustainability credential, and EarthAware products deliver that without any decoration trade-off.

For maximum sustainability alignment, consider eco-friendly decoration methods: water-based inks for screen print, recycled-content thread for embroidery, or organic cotton woven patches. Some decorators now offer carbon-offset decoration services. Pair these with an EarthAware product and the entire end-to-end story strengthens — from certified raw material through responsible decoration to polybag-free delivery.
