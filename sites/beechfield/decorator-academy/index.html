Every Beechfield product is engineered with decoration in mind. Explore techniques, placement guides, and expert tutorials to get the best results from our headwear.

Decoration Techniques

Choose Your Method

Our headwear is compatible with all major decoration techniques. Each method has its strengths — find the right one for your project.

✍

Embroidery

The gold standard for headwear decoration. Direct embroidery delivers premium, durable results on structured and unstructured caps alike. Compatible with all Beechfield headwear.

Most Popular
☐

Patches

Woven, PVC, leather, and embroidered patches. Versatile placement across front, side, and back panels. Sew-on recommended for textured fabrics.
⚙

Transfer

Heat transfer for full-colour, photographic designs. Compatible with flat-panel and structured styles. Check time, temperature, and pressure guides per product.
☷

Screen Print

Bold, durable prints on flat surfaces. Ideal for promotional runs and event headwear. Best on beanies, flat-panel caps, and accessories.
⎙

DTG (Direct to Garment)

Digital printing directly onto fabric. Excellent for complex, multi-colour designs on cotton and cotton-blend headwear.
◆

Sublimation

All-over print capability on polyester headwear. Vibrant, permanent colour that becomes part of the fabric. Best on white and light-coloured polyester.
