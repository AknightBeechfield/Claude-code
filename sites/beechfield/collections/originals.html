Beechfield Collection

Originals

The classics that built the brand.

The collection that started everything.

The Originals collection is Beechfield at its purest. Classic shapes, reliable fabrics, proven decoration performance. Every style in this collection has been in the range for years — because they keep selling, keep performing, and keep coming back in new colours.

These are the caps buyers trust when a customer needs a reliable, repeatable product with no surprises. Consistent sizing, consistent quality, consistent results — order after order.

Reliable. Repeatable. Proven.

Originals styles are the workhorses of the Beechfield catalogue. Built to accept high-volume embroidery, screen printing and transfer decoration with results that hold wash after wash.

When your customers need confidence in a cap — in the shape, the weight, the finish — Originals deliver every time.

What defines the Originals collection

  • Heavy brushed cotton construction
  • Structured, pre-curved profiles
  • 60+ colour options across the range
  • Consistent sizing run to run
  • Proven decoration compatibility
  • Available in adult and junior fits
