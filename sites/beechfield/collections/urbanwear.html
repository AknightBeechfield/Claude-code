Beechfield Collection

Urbanwear

Street-ready. Brand-ready.

Built for the city. Designed for the brand.

Urbanwear is built for the city — structured profiles, bold colour blocking and silhouettes that make a statement before a single logo is applied. Snapbacks, truckers and flat-peak styles designed with decoration in mind.

These are the styles that get noticed. The caps that stop the scroll, start the conversation and build brand identity at street level.

Snapbacks, truckers, flat peaks.

From classic foam-front truckers to structured snapback profiles, Urbanwear covers the full spectrum of statement headwear. Each style is engineered for embroidery and print application — large front panels, clean surfaces, solid foundations.

  • High-profile structured front panels
  • Snapback and fitted adjusters
  • Foam-front trucker construction
  • Flat and curved peak options
  • Bold colour-blocking across the range

Made for decoration

Large front panels, structured crowns and smooth fabric surfaces make Urbanwear styles ideal for embroidery, woven badges, screen printing and heat transfer decoration. Your logo, amplified.
