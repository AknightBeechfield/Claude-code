Decoration Guide for Outdoor Headwear

Outdoor headwear faces unique decoration challenges. Fleece fabrics require stabiliser backing for clean embroidery. Technical mesh panels are best suited to heat-transfer or woven patch application rather than direct embroidery. Knitted beanies respond well to large-format embroidery on the cuff — keep designs simple with bold thread weight for maximum visibility at distance.

For caps used in rain-prone environments, consider embroidery over print — thread is inherently waterproof and will not crack or peel with repeated wetting. If print is required, transfer methods outperform direct screen print on performance fabrics.
