The Shape Guide

From baseball to bucket hat — understand every silhouette in the Beechfield range.

Why Shape Matters

Choose the Right Silhouette for Your Brand

The shape of a cap is the foundation of any headwear branding programme. It determines the decoration area available, the aesthetic of the finished product, and ultimately how the wearer connects with your brand. Whether you need the generous front panel of a classic baseball cap for bold embroidery, the relaxed charm of a dad hat for lifestyle branding, or the streetwear credibility of a flat peak snapback — choosing the right silhouette is the first and most important decision you will make. This guide breaks down every shape in the Beechfield range to help you make that choice with confidence.

Baseball Cap

Baseball Cap

The classic 6-panel structured cap with a pre-curved or flat peak. The most popular silhouette for branding, offering a generous front panel for embroidery and print.

Key Features Structured crown, Pre-curved peak, Adjustable back (snapback/buckle/velcro)
Best For
Corporate Sports Promotional
Decoration Areas Front panel (60x40mm), Side panels, Back
B010 B015 B57 B691
Trucker Cap

Trucker Cap

A structured front with mesh back panels for breathability. The foam front panel provides an excellent flat surface for decoration.

Key Features Foam front, Mesh back, Snapback closure
Best For
Outdoor Casual Events
Decoration Areas Front foam panel, Side panel
B630 B640
Dad Hat

Dad Hat

An unstructured, relaxed-fit cap with a pre-curved peak. Soft and comfortable, with a vintage aesthetic popular in fashion and lifestyle branding.

Key Features Unstructured crown, Pre-curved peak, Buckle/strap closure
Best For
Fashion Lifestyle Vintage
Decoration Areas Front panel, Side
B59 B653 B688
Flat Peak

Flat Peak

A structured cap with a flat, uncurved peak. Bold and urban, perfect for street-style branding and embroidery programmes.

Key Features Structured crown, Flat peak, Snapback closure
Best For
Urban Street Youth
Decoration Areas Front panel, Side panel, Peak underside
B610 B660
Army / Cadet

Army / Cadet

A flat-topped, round-crowned cap inspired by military style. Distinctive silhouette with a short, curved peak.

Key Features Flat top, Short peak, No front panel seam
Best For
Workwear Military-inspired Fashion
Decoration Areas Front (centre), Side
B33 B34
