New Season

Heads Up for SS26

SS26 embraces headwear innovation with a sharp nod to contemporary styling, introducing 11 new products and 17 fresh colours. Classic shapes are reworked with clean lines, confident materials and considered comfort, from relaxed lifestyle fits to performance-led styles. Designed to wear now, sell with confidence and brand with consistency.

Collection

Essentially Organic

EarthAware® reworks classic silhouettes in 100% organic, OCS-certified cotton, delivering a relaxed, considered approach to everyday headwear with comfort and conscious design at its core.

Lifestyle

Effortless Cool

Clean, relaxed and confidently understated styles for everyday wear. Unstructured crowns, semi-curved peaks and classic trucker mesh deliver an easy edge, with lightweight fedoras and EarthAware® organic cotton options rounding out the season.

Performance

Form Meets Function

Performance-ready headwear with comfort at the core. Lightweight fabrics, breathable construction and weather-ready options, designed for training, events and everyday motion. Reliable, versatile and engineered with purpose.
