Events

Find us, meet us, talk headwear.

Why Visit Beechfield at a Trade Show?

Trade shows are where our products come to life. Step away from the screen, pick up the product, and experience the quality, weight and finish that makes Beechfield the go-to headwear brand for decorators and distributors across Europe. Whether you are looking to expand your catalogue, source new decoration-ready blanks, or simply get inspired by the latest seasonal styles, our stand is your destination.

Hands-On Product Experience

See and feel the full Beechfield range in person. Compare fabrics, constructions and colourways side by side — something no online swatch can replace. From our signature Original Cuffed Beanie to the latest cord and organic cotton caps, every product is there for you to examine.

Meet the Team

Talk directly with our design and sales experts. Whether you have questions about product specifications, minimum order quantities, or custom colour matching, our team is on hand to help you find exactly what you need for your business.

New Season Previews

Get a first look at upcoming styles before they hit the catalogue. Trade shows are where we reveal new collections, seasonal colour palettes and product innovations — giving you a head start on planning your next range.

Expert Decoration Consultations

Get advice on the best products for your decoration method. Our team can guide you through embroidery-optimised panels, transfer-friendly fabrics and patch-ready constructions to ensure your finished product looks its best.

Partner Opportunities

Interested in distribution, exclusive lines or bespoke projects? Our commercial team is available at every show to discuss partnership opportunities, volume programmes and tailored solutions for your market.
