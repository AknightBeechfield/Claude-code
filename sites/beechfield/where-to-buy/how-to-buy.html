How to Buy Beechfield Products

Four simple steps from browsing to branded headwear — choose, connect, order and decorate.

Your Buying Journey

Whether you are ordering for the first time or restocking a best-seller, the process is straightforward. Follow these four steps to go from blank headwear to branded product.

Step 1 — Choose Your Product

Browse our full range of 250+ products. Use filters to narrow by type (caps, beanies, bucket hats), colour, decoration method, collection or sustainability credentials. Every product page includes full specifications, available colourways and decoration area details.

Not sure where to start? Our Collections pages group products by purpose and use-case — from corporate and workwear to streetwear and outdoor.

Browse Products
250+ Products

Step 2 — Find a Distributor

Beechfield products are sold exclusively through authorised distributors. Use our distributor finder to search by region, specialism or product type. Each distributor holds stock locally, so you benefit from fast delivery and dedicated support.

If you already have a distributor account, you can go directly to them for pricing and availability. If you are new, our finder tool will help you identify the best partner for your needs.

Find a Distributor
UK & European Network

Step 3 — Place Your Order

Contact your chosen distributor to place your order. They will confirm stock availability, pricing, lead times and delivery options. Most distributors offer next-day delivery on stocked items, and many provide online ordering portals for repeat customers.

You can check real-time stock levels before ordering using our Stock Checker tool, which shows availability by style, colour and size across the distributor network.

Next-Day Delivery

Step 4 — Decorate

Every Beechfield product is designed decoration-first. Whether you are embroidering, printing, applying transfers or using DTG, our blanks are built to deliver clean, professional results across all major decoration methods.

Visit the Decorator Academy for technique guides, decoration area templates and best-practice advice for each product type. Our Cap Studio tool also lets you visualise your branding before committing to production.

Decoration-First Design

Ready to Order?

Find your nearest authorised distributor and get started today. Our network covers the UK and Europe with local stock and next-day delivery.
