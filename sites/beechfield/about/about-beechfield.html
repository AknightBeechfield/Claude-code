About / Our Story

Original Headwear Since 1994

Thirty years of craft, credibility and caps built to be decorated.

How We Got Here

1994

Founded

Beechfield is established with a single mission: build headwear that decorators can rely on.

2000s

Range Expansion

We grow from caps into beanies, hats and seasonal headwear — always keeping decoration at the core.

2010s

EarthAware Launched

Our certified organic and recycled range launches, reflecting a commitment to better materials without compromise on performance.

2020s+

Digital Transformation

New tools, platforms and resources make it easier than ever for decorators and brand managers to work with us.

What Original Headwear Means

Built to carry someone else's story.

Original doesn't mean first. It means authentic. Every product we make is designed to be decorated — to carry a story that isn't ours. We build headwear that becomes someone else's brand, someone else's identity. That's been the principle since day one.
