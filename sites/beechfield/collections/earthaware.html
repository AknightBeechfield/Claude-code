EarthAware®

Organic. Certified. Considered.

OCS Certified Organic Cotton

The same shapes. A lower footprint.

EarthAware® is Beechfield's organic cotton collection — reworking the classic shapes you know in 100% organic cotton, certified to OCS standards. Same decoration-ready construction. Same reliability. Lower environmental impact.

Every style in the EarthAware® range is built to the same performance standards as the rest of the Beechfield catalogue — suitable for embroidery, printing and all major decoration methods.

What OCS certified means

The Organic Content Standard (OCS) verifies the presence and amount of organic material in a final product. Every EarthAware® product is independently verified — from raw fibre through to finished headwear.

OCS certification gives decorators and their customers a credible, third-party-verified claim to support sustainability positioning — without compromising on product quality or decoration performance.

Beyond the classic cap.

EarthAware® isn't limited to five-panel caps. The collection spans bucket hats, studio caps and structured profiles — giving decorators a full range of organic cotton options to present to their customers.

Natural tones and earthy colourways make EarthAware® styles a natural fit for lifestyle, outdoor and wellness brands.
