The Story

The reliable all-rounder. Season after season.

The B10 is Beechfield's most versatile cap. A mid-profile, six-panel structured cap in heavy brushed cotton, it's the cap that works for everyone — from sports clubs to corporate giveaways to streetwear. Available in 30+ solid colours with hook-and-loop fastening, it fits comfortably across adult head sizes. The large front panel is an excellent canvas for embroidery, and the consistent construction means predictable decoration results every time.

  • 100% heavy brushed cotton
  • Six-panel structured design
  • Pre-curved peak
  • Hook-and-loop closure
  • 30+ colourways
  • One size fits most adults
  • TearAway label

Built for Decoration

Built for reliable, repeatable decoration.

Embroidery

The firm cotton front of the B10 gives a reliable backing for machine embroidery across all 30+ colours. Consistent construction means predictable results whether you're doing 10 or 10,000 pieces.

Screen Print & Transfer

Cotton accepts screen print and heat transfer without fuss. The B10's mid-profile crown gives you a generous print area — ideal for bold logos and multi-colour designs.

Patches

Woven patches sit clean and flat on the B10's structured front panel. Popular for sports teams, schools, and workwear — anywhere consistency and volume matter.
