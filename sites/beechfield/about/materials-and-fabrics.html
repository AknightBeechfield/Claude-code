Materials & Fabrics

Plain English. No greenwash. Just the facts.

What We Use and Why

Every fibre choice we make is deliberate. Here's an honest breakdown.

Base Fibre

Cotton

Our most used base fibre. Breathable, durable, takes dye consistently. The backbone of the decorated headwear industry for good reason.

EarthAware Range

Organic Cotton (OCS)

The same performance, with certified organic credentials. Used across the EarthAware® range — every batch third-party verified to OCS standard.

Recycled Materials

Recycled Polyester

Made from post-consumer plastic. Lightweight, moisture-wicking, lower impact. GRS certified where it matters most.

Structured Caps

Brushed Cotton Twill

The standard for structured caps. Smooth surface, holds shape, decoration-ready. The go-to for clean embroidery results.

Performance

Polyester & Nylon

Lightweight, moisture-wicking, quick-drying. Ideal for sports headwear and outdoor activewear. Holds colour vibrancy wash after wash and resists pilling. Typically 100–120 GSM.

Technical

Ripstop

Reinforced grid-weave construction prevents tears from spreading. Used in our outdoor and military-style caps. Durable, lightweight, and highly resistant to abrasion.

Premium Texture

Corduroy

Rich texture with a vintage feel. The distinctive ribbed surface adds visual depth and tactile quality. Pairs well with embroidery and woven patches for a premium finish.

Breathable

Mesh

Open-weave construction for maximum airflow. Used in trucker-style rear panels and performance caps. Provides ventilation without sacrificing structure.

Knit Construction

Acrylic Knit

Double-knit construction provides warmth, shape retention, and excellent embroidery stability. The foundation of our beanie and winter accessories range. Soft handle, colour-fast, easy care.

Premium Knitwear

Fleece & Suprafleece™

Anti-pill fleece for warmth without bulk. Our Suprafleece range delivers superior softness with enhanced colour retention. Ideal for embroidered corporate and outdoor headwear.

Certifications Explained

What "recycled" and "organic" actually mean for us.

OCS (Organic Content Standard) verifies that organic cotton was grown without prohibited pesticides and synthetic fertilisers, and tracks it through the supply chain to the finished product.

GRS (Global Recycled Standard) verifies the recycled content of a product and its supply chain — from raw material through to finished goods. When we say recycled, we mean certified.
